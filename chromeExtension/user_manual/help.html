0. Commit.

Welcome! To get the best results of beating your addiction/impulse/whatever it may be, there are some suggestions I have for you.

First, I suggest that you keep in mind what an addiction to anything is:
An addiction to some entity A develops as one continues to find pleasure in the entity A. As more and more pleasure is received, the brain will strengthen more and more neurons that associate pleasure with A. As a result: The stronger the associations with A, the greater pull one feels towards A when s/he isn't using A.
An example: When you are hungry, your brain provides thoughts of food and eating. This is because the pains of hunger transform into pleasure when the body receives food. The hungrier one is, the more thoughts s/he will have about food and consumption, as humans are directly addicted to eating (because not eating results in death).

My next suggestion is to spend more time away from your computer and phone when you don't need to be on them.
In my experience, spending time with family, friends, outdoors, and with people in my free time helped me escape thoughts regarding porn and porn use. Of course, when I have to use my computer for homework and etc, I try to focus on my homework. When a distracting thought appears, I'll stop what I'm doing and go talk to a friend or get away from my computer for a bit.

In the case that you feel tempted to visit porn while on your phone, visit instead the sites you've saved in PorNo! on your computer. This may help redirect your energy and thoughts to the same level as your computer does.

Spend more time with members of your desired sexual preference (ie I am a heterosexual male, so my desired preference of gender is female).
By interacting with more "real people" of the gender, you dissolve your brain's strong images of porn associated with the desired gender. This is very helpful with overcoming the false ideas portrayed in porn, resulting in giving you a better understanding of the people in the world. Additionally, you'll meet some very cool people along the way, and your brain will rewire its pleasure receptors with the appreciation of individuals beyond surface level, which weakens the pull that porn has on your mind and possibly helping you make some super neat friends!

Finally, commit. Make a resolution and resolve it. Fail unwillingly, succeed willingly.
Treat this journey with the discipline you would if you trained for your favorite sport/hobby/profession/etc.
The effects of porn use are quite terrible, and the benefits of overcoming them are quite terrific. You won't miss out on anything once you've stopped filling your time with porn -- in fact, you'll find yourself spending that time on things you enjoy and bring you greater joy.
This process of this detox is tough, but not impossible. Every time you relapse, note down what contributed to you relapsing. Learn how to avoid those factors, and try again. The more you learn about the habits you've created and how to avoid them, the better you'll get at overcoming your urges. If it helps, get a friend to join you on your journey to help serve as extra motivation.

2. Copy the website address (http://www.example.com) and paste it into the input box above the add button in the PorNo! menu.

Adding a name in the "Name this link?" input box will display the title in the menu instead of the address to make the menu easier to read. If this box is left blank, the website's address is displayed. Now, click the add button!

3. Adding local files.

You can add files on your computer too! Just open a file in your browser (for example, right-click on an image saved to your desktop, select "Open with Chrome", and the image should open in your browser window). Copy the file path in the search bar, paste it into PorNo!, name the link if you desire, and select add. That's how you add files!

4. To delete links, simply click the x in the rightmost side of each link.

5. The EMERGENCY button.

The EMERGENCY button is there for the times when you feel that you could use a little bit more help. Clicking it opens all of the links you've saved, so that you can view all the things that inspire you, empower you, define you...as a gentle reminder of what's important.

6. Receiving updates.

There are two ways to receive updates!
The first way is to do nothing. As I push updates to PorNo!, your version of PorNo! will update automatically!
The second way is to open the PorNo! menu for a few seconds every now and then. Due to the way PorNo! is written, PorNo! is allowed to communicate with the PorNo! database only when the menu is open. By opening the menu for 5 to 10 seconds once every few days, you allow your copy of PorNo! to download the latest links straight from the database.

Extra: For those who want to use PorNo! anonymously for whatever reason,

Right-click on the PorNo! icon (yellow heart) and select "Hide in Chrome Menu". To access PorNo!, select the row of three vertical dots in the rightmost side of your browser (to the right of the website address bar) and select the PorNo! icon. PorNo!'s menu will appear for full use, but will disappear back into hiding when you're finished using it. All the functionality of PorNo!, tucked away for your desired privacy!
To bring the icon back, right-click on the icon while in the menu and select "Keep in toolbar".
